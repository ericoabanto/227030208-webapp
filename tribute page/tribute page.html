Michael Jackson

The King of Pop

Here's a time line of Michael Jackson's life:

  • 1958 - Born in Gary, Indiana
  • 1964 - Begins performing with the Jackson 5
  • 1979 - Releases "Off the Wall," his first solo album as an adult
  • 1982 - "Thriller" album released, becomes the best-selling album of all time
  • 1983 - Moonwalk debuts on Motown 25: Yesterday, Today, Forever
  • 1991 - Releases "Dangerous" album
  • 1993 - Accused of child sexual abuse
  • 2009 - Dies at the age of 50

"Michael Jackson's influence on music, dance, and fashion is unparalleled. He was a global icon whose legacy continues to inspire generations."

-- Madonna
